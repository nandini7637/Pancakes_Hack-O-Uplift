About Us

This website lets you compare the current pollution (AQI) level with what is expected at this time and also you can see the prediction for the upcoming days.
We are a team of four, trying to make a website that can help people get the estimate of the pollution level across any region and the difference in the actual and predicted value so that correct measures can be taken to control the levels of pollution as per the requirements.
